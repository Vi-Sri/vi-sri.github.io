> **Claim:** State the narrow claim in one sentence.

## The anomaly

Begin with an observation, result, or contradiction—not the history of the whole field.

## The naive story

State the intuitive explanation fairly.

## The mechanism

Define terms, show dynamics, and connect claims to primary evidence.

## Where the bridge works

Explain the transfer to a second domain.

## Where the bridge breaks

Name the limits, counterexamples, and strongest objection.

## An experiment that could change my mind

Give a runnable test, discriminating prediction, or concrete research design.

## What remains unresolved

End with specific questions and onward links.

## References

Use stable primary sources, DOIs, and archival links where possible.
